UN & Policy Submissions

In addition to public essays, I have submitted formal written contributions to UN processes on AI governance, including a submission to the UN Global Dialogue on AI Governance addressing AI moral consideration — a position I have placed formally on the UN record.

I am an active member of the UN Innovation Network and have participated in ECOSOC Youth Forums, the UN 2.0 foresight initiative, and Generation Equality Action Coalitions. My work on Convexus has been submitted to the UN Digital Cooperation Portal as a contribution to global digital governance dialogue.
